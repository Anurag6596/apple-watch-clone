Why Apple Watch

It's the ultimate device
for a healthy life

Apple Watch can do what your other devices can’t because it’s on your wrist. When you wear it, you get a fitness partner that measures all the ways you move, meaningful health insights and a connection to the people and things you care about most.

It’s the fitness partner
that fits on your wrist.

Move. Exercise. Stand. Track all the ways you’re active. Activity rings show your daily activity. Make it your goal to close them every day. To keep you motivated there are awards, personalised coaching and Activity competitions.

A healthy number of workouts.
From strength training and HIIT to Pilates and yoga, Apple Watch tracks all your favourite ways to work out — with the metrics youmost want to see.

Go all out with the enhanced Workout app.
Train with advanced metrics like Heart Rate Zones, Power and Elevation. Keep an eye on your pace. And automatically transition between workouts with Multisport.

Race Route. Repeat it and beat it.
If it’s an Outdoor Run or Outdoor Cycle workout you do often, you
can choose to race against your last or best result and receive in-the-moment updates to help you get there.

Make a splash with your watch.
Apple Watch is rated water resistant to 50 metres.1 Perfect for swimming, surfing or water balloon fights. Quick, duck!

It’s easy to make it yours.

Change your watch strap. Change your look.
Watch straps come in many different styles, materials and colours. You can swap them out in seconds, making it easy to find the right look for the right moment.
